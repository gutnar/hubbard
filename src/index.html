Intructions

Iteration calculations:
* All numbers must be floats and must contain a dot.
* Use sq to square and cb to cube a number.
* When the input is not correctly formatted, the image will not update.

Increasing the iterations count increases rendering time. Setting iterations too high will freeze the application.

Fade shows how far on the complex plane the iteration result must be from its closest root to draw a completely black pixel.
